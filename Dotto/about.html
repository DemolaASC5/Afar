Afar was created by a group of high school students who wanted to create a place for other high school students to be able to find study abroad programs that are safe, fit their academic purposes as well as their financial needs. We chose a dog as our mascot because we wanted to release our website in the Chinese year of the dog. We chose the name Afar because it's easy to remember and it's simple.
